# JobFlow

## Introduction

JobFlow is an AI-powered job application management platform designed to help job seekers organize applications, schedules, and preparation materials in one place.

The platform automatically tracks application statuses, stores submitted resumes and cover letters, extracts information from images and social media posts, and prioritizes tasks based on the user's goals.

## Key Features
# Application Tracking
Manage job applications in a single dashboard.
Mark applications as completed and track their progress.
Store submitted resumes and cover letters for future reference.
# AI Schedule Extraction
Extract schedules, deadlines, times, and event details from screenshots.
Import information from social media posts and links automatically.
Organize recruitment events, lectures, and career-related activities into a unified calendar.
# Content Collection
Save useful recruitment information from social media platforms.
Automatically collect and summarize relevant content from linked pages.
# Priority Management
Drag and drop tasks to reorder priorities.
Pin important tasks to keep them fixed at the top of the list.
Focus on the most important applications and preparation activities.
# Integrated Job Preparation Workspace
Manage applications, schedules, study plans, and career events in one place.
Reduce manual organization and improve productivity during the job search process.

# Usage
1. Sign in to JobFlow.
2. Create a new job application entry.
3. Upload screenshots or paste links from social media posts.
4. Let the AI extract schedules and important information automatically.
5. Organize and prioritize your tasks using drag-and-drop controls.
6. Track your application progress and review saved documents anytime.

# Future Enhancements
- Browser extension for automatic application detection.
- AI-powered cover letter analysis and feedback.
- Interview preparation assistant.
- Personalized job recommendations.
- Synchronization with external calendar services.
